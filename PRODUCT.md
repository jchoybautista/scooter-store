# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Store admin and staff at Velocità — managing day-to-day operations: reviewing orders, updating product listings, monitoring branch activity, and managing customer records. Used at a desk but increasingly on mobile when checking orders on the go.

## Product Purpose

The Velocità Admin Panel is an internal operations tool for the scooter store. It gives staff a single place to manage orders, products, customers, and settings. Success means tasks complete quickly with minimal friction — the interface should disappear into the workflow.

## Brand Personality

Precise, efficient, modern. The admin carries the Velocità brand (Italian premium, orange accent, dark navy) into a utility surface — refined but purposeful. Design serves speed, not impression.

## Anti-references

Not Shopify or any generic e-commerce admin template. Avoid the beige/cream dashboard default, cookie-cutter sidebar + topbar chrome, and the "SaaS product tour" aesthetic. Also avoid enterprise bloat (heavy tables, excessive chrome, cluttered toolbars).

## Design Principles

1. **Disappear into the task.** The interface should never slow the user down. Every click, tap, and scroll should feel effortless.
2. **Mobile is a first-class context.** Admins check orders on the go. Touch targets, drawer navigation, and stacked layouts are not afterthoughts.
3. **Consistency over surprise.** Same component vocabulary across every screen. If a button looks different in two places, one is wrong.
4. **Information density on demand.** Dense tables on desktop; progressive disclosure on mobile. Never hide core functionality.
5. **Brand in restraint.** The carrot orange earns its place on primary actions and active states only — not decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA baseline. 44×44px minimum touch targets on mobile. Focus indicators always visible. Sufficient contrast on all text including muted/dim variants.
